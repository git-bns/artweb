About the Artist

Prativa is a contemporary Indian artist known for her vibrant paintings that blend traditional Indian art forms with modern techniques. Based in India, she draws inspiration from rich cultural heritage, nature, and spiritual themes.

Her work explores themes of tradition, spirituality, and contemporary Indian life through bold colors, intricate patterns, and meaningful symbolism that resonates with both traditional and modern art lovers.
