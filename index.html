About the Artist

Elena Rodriguez is a contemporary artist based in New York, known for her vibrant paintings and innovative mixed media works. With over 15 years of experience, her art has been featured in galleries across the United States and Europe.

Her work explores themes of human connection, nature, and the urban experience through bold colors and dynamic compositions.
